Getting Started

New to StitchGrid? Here's how to create your first stitch chart.

1

Upload or generate an image

Choose a photo from your library, or type a description to let AI create one for you.

2

Set your grid size

Choose from 10×10 to 50×50 to match your project's detail and yarn weight.

3

Generate the chart

Tap "Generate Stitch Chart" and your colour-mapped chart appears instantly.

4

Follow with Focus Mode

Activate Focus Mode to highlight your current row and track progress as you stitch.

Frequently Asked Questions

StitchGrid accepts JPEG, PNG, HEIC, and most standard formats from your iPhone photo library. Clear images with a distinct subject work best.
You can choose from 10×10 to 50×50. Smaller grids suit simple motifs; larger ones work for detailed projects.
Focus Mode highlights your current row while dimming all others. Tap any row number to activate it, and use the row selector to jump to any position in the chart.
Saved stitch charts are viewable offline. Generating charts and using the AI generator both require an internet connection.
