Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025 · Effective immediately

StitchGrid is designed with your privacy at its core. We do not collect personal data, we do not track you, and your stitch charts stay on your device.

1. No Personal Data Collection

StitchGrid does not require an account, name, email address, or any personally identifying information. We do not collect, store, or share personal data.

2. Data That Stays on Your Device

Your stitch charts, app preferences, and project data are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad and never leave your device unless you explicitly share them.

  • Stitch charts and generated grids — stored locally
  • App settings and preferences — stored locally
  • Uploaded images — processed locally, not retained on servers

3. AI Image Generation

How AI Generation Works

StitchGrid uses a third-party AI service to generate images based on user input. When you enter a prompt, the text is sent to this service to generate the requested image. No personal information is required to use this feature. The data shared is limited to the text prompt provided by you.

What is shared

Only the text prompt you type (e.g. "chubby smiling puppy") is sent to the AI service — used solely to generate the image, and not linked to any personal identity.

What is not shared

  • Your name or identity
  • Your device information
  • Your location
  • Any other personal data

Third-party AI provider

The AI image generation service is provided by a third party. Their handling of prompt data is governed by their own privacy policy.

4. Image Uploads

When you upload a photo to generate a stitch chart, the image is processed to produce your chart. Uploaded images are not stored on our servers.

5. No Tracking or Analytics

StitchGrid does not use advertising trackers, third-party analytics SDKs, or any cross-app tracking technology. We do not build a profile of your behaviour or share data with advertisers.

6. In-App Purchases

Any purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

7. Children's Privacy

StitchGrid is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will update the date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

9. Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@stitchgrid.app.
